Episode 1: Jaanaki

When an opportunity to go to the Gulf arises, Nagendran finds himself short of funds. Soman proposes an unconventional solution: marriage.

Episode 2: Lillikutty

Nagendran and Soman arrive in Pathanamthitta to see Lillykutty. While Lillykutty weaves her influence over Nagendran, can he secure Varkey's elusive approval?

Episode 3: Laila

Nagendran and Soman encounter Laila in Kasaragod. Her mysterious past ensnares Nagendran with fear. Can he break free from her grasp?

Episode 4: Saavithri

Nagendran and Soman reach Palakkad to acquire the necessary funds and meet Saavithri. Can Nagendran save her, or will he get trapped in the palls of a dilapidated Brahmin household?

Episode 5: Thangam

Why are the locals rushing to marry off Thangam? Nagendran meets Thangam in Kuttanad and struggles to outwit the locals. He and Soman try to escape from Varkey's grasp.

Episode 6: Mozhi

Bewitched by Mozhi at first sight, Nagendran is not able to deceive her. Tensions rise as he and Soman clash for the first time. Can Nagendran forge a new life with Mozhi?
